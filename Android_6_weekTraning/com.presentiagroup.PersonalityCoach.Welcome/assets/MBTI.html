Personality Guide can help you to choose your profile and also for your partner, friend or colleagues. Imagine getting immediate access at any time to information to help you better understand and communicate with your colleagues. Get personalized tips and do's and don'ts for interacting with friends, family, and coworkers with different preferences from your own. It will give you some personality tips and how you can improve your personality, behaviour and your communication. It will connect you to your friends always and you can match your profile with the other one. .

You can access following things via Personality Guide.
*Enter and update your own type.
*Create your own Type Database by entering the names and types of your colleagues, family, and friends
*Get personalized communication tips for interacting with each person in your database.
*Get communication tips for each type working with one another.
*Browse and make revisions to your database as needed.
*You get immediate access to detailed information about all 16 types. For each type you get:
* overall description
* at work
* in general and intimate relationships
* parenting
* conflict style .
