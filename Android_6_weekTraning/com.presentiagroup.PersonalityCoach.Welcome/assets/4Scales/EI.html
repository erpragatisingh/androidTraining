Ways Extraverts and Introverts Can Interact Better

If you are an EXTRAVERT working with another EXTRAVERT

·         LISTEN!

·         Let the other person talk.

·         Don’t be competitive.

·         Know what you will say and say it. Then, be quiet.

If you are an EXTRAVERT working with an INTROVERT

·         Encourage others to contribute.

·         Resist urge to fill silence with talk.

·         Don’t let silence intimidate you.

·         Give time for others to reflect.

·         Don’t confuse silence with consent.

If you are an INTROVERT working with another INTROVERT

·         Be prepared to take the lead in talking.

·         Don’t be afraid to interrupt a silence after Reflection.

·         Don’t confuse silence with consent.

If you are an INTROVERT Working with an EXTRAVERT

·         Be prepared to share.

·         Be prepared to be more verbal than you may prefer.

·         Assert yourself in volume and words.

·         Don’t be afraid to interject.
