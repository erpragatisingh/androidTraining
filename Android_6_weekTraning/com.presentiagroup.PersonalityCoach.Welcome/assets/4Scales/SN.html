Ways Sensors and iNutitives Can Interact Better

If you are a SENSOR working with another SENSOR

·         Work on restating the obvious in general terms.

·         Share your dreams with each other.

·         Try to keep each other from always interpreting so literally what’s being said.

If you are a SENSOR working with a iNTUITIVE

·         Keep in mind that whatever you say, your partner will find different ways to interpret it.

·         Check interpretations and meanings so you are satisfied with what’s been said.

·         Keep in mind that your colleague may naturally give a general response to your specific statement.

If you are a iNTUITIVE working with another iNTUITIVE

·         Be sure to check out each other’s meanings about what is being said.

·         Allow time for each of you to stop, look, listen, smell, feel, or taste.  Don’t be afraid to let your senses become overloaded.

·         Translate what the other is saying into specifics: who, what, where, when, and why.

If you are a iNTUITIVE working with a SENSOR

·         Remember that much of what you say-and don’t say-will be taken literally.

·         Appreciate the specific and grounded help your colleague gives to what you say.

·         Remember that your partner prefers precision and facts before he or she can formulate a response.
